# US-08 — See my cue when it's my turn

**Epic:** Practice Session

## User Story
As an actor, I want the app to clearly signal when it's time for me to speak so that I don't lose my place in the run.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Cue card displays: the preceding line (for context) + a visible "Your line" prompt
- Distinct visual treatment (color, layout) separating cue from other lines
